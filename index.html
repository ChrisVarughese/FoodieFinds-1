Prepare a filling by mixing boiled and mashed potatoes with peas, onions, garlic, ginger, and spices. Spoon the filling onto samosa wrappers or phyllo pastry sheets, fold them into triangles, and seal the edges. Deep fry or bake the samosas until they're golden and crispy. Serve hot with chutney or sauce for a delicious snack or appetizer.

Niena Nisanth

Heat oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add chopped vegetables (such as carrots, peas, bell peppers) and cook until slightly softened. Stir in cooked rice and soy sauce, tossing to combine. Push the rice to one side of the pan and crack an egg into the empty space, scrambling until cooked. Mix the egg into the rice, season with salt and pepper to taste, and garnish with green onions or cilantro before serving. Enjoy!

Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). Roll out store-bought or homemade pizza dough onto a baking sheet. Spread pizza sauce over the dough, then add desired toppings such as cheese, pepperoni, vegetables, and herbs. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly. Enjoy!
